Be friendly to the guide dog.
  • It is okay to ask someone if you may pet their guide.
  • A Guide Dog should never be offered food or other distracting treats.
  • Calling out to a Guide Dog or intentionally obstructing its path can be dangerous for the team.
  • It's not all work and no play for a Guide Dog.
  • A Guide Dog can make mistakes and will need reminders to maintain its training.
  • Before asking a question of a person handling a dog, allow them to complete the task at hand.
